About the Business Department

The Business Department at King Saud University is part of the College of Business Administration, established in 1959. The department has a long history of educating business professionals, managers, entrepreneurs, and leaders, contributing significantly to the development of the business sector in Saudi Arabia and beyond.

History

The Business Department has been a pioneer in business education at King Saud University, offering comprehensive programs that cover various aspects of business and management.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution in business education, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and excellence in the field of business and management.

Academic Programs

We offer a range of academic programs, including undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various business disciplines. Our programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for successful careers in business.

Faculty Members

Our faculty members are distinguished scholars and practitioners in the field of business, dedicated to providing high-quality education and mentorship to our students.

Research Areas

The Business Department is actively involved in research across various areas of business, contributing to the development of business knowledge and practice.
