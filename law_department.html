About the Law Department

The Law Department at King Saud University is part of the College of Law and Political Sciences, established in 1427 AH. The department has a rich history of over forty years, offering high-quality academic programs and continuously updating its study plans to meet modern standards.

History

The Law Department has been a cornerstone of legal education at King Saud University, providing comprehensive programs that cover various aspects of law and legal studies.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution in legal education, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and excellence in the field of law.

Academic Programs

We offer a range of academic programs, including undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various legal disciplines. Our programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for successful careers in law.

Faculty Members

Our faculty members are distinguished scholars and practitioners in the field of law, dedicated to providing high-quality education and mentorship to our students.

Research Areas

The Law Department is actively involved in research across various areas of law, contributing to the development of legal knowledge and practice.
